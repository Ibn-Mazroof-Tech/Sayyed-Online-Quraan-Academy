About Sayyed Online Qur'aan Academy

About the Founder

My name is Sayyed Muhammad Areeb, Founder of Sayyed Online Qur'aan Academy. I am a passionate and dedicated Quran teacher committed to spreading authentic Islamic knowledge. I am a Haafiz, 'Aalim, and Faazil, and currently serving as an Islamic Researcher at Jamia Hamdard University.

With sincerity, patience, and responsibility, my aim is to create a respectful and motivating learning environment for students of all ages. My mission is to ensure that every student learns the Qur'aan correctly with Tajweed and develops a strong spiritual connection with it.

Our Mission

To provide structured and accessible Quran education with proper pronunciation (Makharij), Tajweed rules, and understanding.

Our Vision

To build a generation that not only recites the Quran beautifully but also practices its teachings in everyday life.
